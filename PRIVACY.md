# Privacy & Terms

_The in-app version lives at [`public/privacy.html`](public/privacy.html) and is
linked from the app footer. This file is the plain-text mirror._

## The short version

drift is a sound mixer that runs entirely in your browser. It has no accounts,
no servers of its own, and no analytics or tracking. It does not collect, store,
or transmit any personal information.

## What stays on your device

- Saved mixes (presets) and the app's current state are kept in your browser's
  local storage, on your device only. They are never uploaded. Clearing your
  browser data removes them.
- All sounds are generated inside the app. Nothing is streamed or downloaded
  from an audio service.

## Third parties

There are none. drift serves everything it needs, including its fonts and
icons, from its own files. It makes no third-party requests, so no outside
service receives your IP address or any request from the app. Once the page
has loaded, it runs entirely offline.

If you install drift from an app store, that store's own privacy terms apply to
the download and your device.

## Children

drift is suitable for all ages and collects nothing from anyone, including
children.

## Terms of use

- drift is provided as-is, for relaxation, focus, and sleep, without warranties
  of any kind.
- drift is **not a medical device** and does not provide medical advice. It is
  not a treatment for any sleep, hearing, or health condition. Speak with a
  qualified professional about any health concerns.
- Keep the volume comfortable; prolonged high-volume listening can harm hearing.

## Changes

Updates appear in this file and in `public/privacy.html`. drift keeps no contact
information, so material changes are reflected on these pages rather than
announced.
