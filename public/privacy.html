drift away

privacy & terms

The short version

drift away is a sound mixer that runs entirely in your browser. It has no accounts, no servers of its own, and no analytics or tracking. It does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information. We never see how you use it.

What stays on your device

  • The mixes you save (presets) and the current state of the app are stored in your browser's local storage, on your device only. They are never uploaded. Clearing your browser data removes them.
  • All sounds are generated inside the app. Nothing is streamed or downloaded from an audio service.

Third parties

drift away loads its fonts and icon set from Google Fonts. When the page loads, your browser requests those files from Google's servers, which means Google may receive your IP address and basic request information, as described in Google's privacy policy. This is the only third-party request drift away makes, and it carries no information about your activity in the app.

If you install drift away from an app store, that store applies its own privacy terms to the download and to your device, separate from this policy.

Children

drift away is suitable for all ages and collects nothing from anyone, including children.

Terms of use

  • drift away is provided as-is, for relaxation, focus, and sleep, without warranties of any kind.
  • drift away is not a medical device and does not provide medical advice. It is not a treatment for any sleep, hearing, or health condition. If you have concerns about your sleep or health, speak with a qualified professional.
  • Please keep the volume at a comfortable level. Prolonged listening at high volume can harm your hearing.

Changes

If this policy changes, the updated version will appear here. Because drift away keeps no contact information, material changes will simply be reflected on this page.
